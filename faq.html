Just simply download the Kidlat Delivery App and register your account. It will give you an option to log-in using your Facebook or Mobile Number.

What are the areas covered for Kidlat Delivery?

We deliver in all municipalities of Bulacan and to other nearby places in Metro Manila.

What items cannot be delivered with Kidlat Delivery?

Liquor, alcohol drinks, drugs, toxic materials, flammable substances, ammunition, guns, sharp objects & jewelries.

What payment method do you accept?

As of now, Kidlat Drivers is accepting payment in Cash or through G-Cash.

What are your operating hours?

As of now, we operate 7 days a week from 8am to 8pm. However, during the Community Quarantine Period, we strictly abide by each city/municipalities’ curfew and restriction on operating hours for delivery services. Check your local government policies for the latest information.

How to be a Kidlat Partner Merchant?

Click “Become a Kidlat Merchant” in our webpage then fill out the form and submit all required documents. Let us review and verify your application and we will contact you as soon as possible.

How to be a Kidlat Partner Rider?

Click “Become a Kidlat Rider” in our webpage then fill out the form and submit all required documents. Let us review and verify your application and we will contact you as soon as possible.
