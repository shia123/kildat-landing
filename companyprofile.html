Company Profile

Introduction

In May 2020, Due of COVID-19 Pandemic led a group of optimistic and generous people to build a mobile delivery service app to help their fellow kababayans by giving them a great delivery satisfactory and we believe starting this business is will assist this new generation with a completely different and competitive style of online food and parcel delivery. The main purpose is to generate income by providing good and efficient services. Also, to add convenience to the lives of people especially during these pandemic times.


Kidlat services are on its way to be known throughout the cities and municipalities of Bulacan and eventually the whole Central Luzon and Calabarzon. By helping local consumers, they don’t need to go out to send parcels at logistics/courier centers and to buy their daily essentials needs. They will have more time to spend with their love ones by providing them great delivery services right at their door steps by using the customer app on their mobile phones/smart phones. helping local merchants to promote their unique businesses and to have additional patronizing customers in different places in Bulacan and nearby areas by using the business mobile app.

Our vision is to be amongst the top leading online food and parcel delivery service companies in the Philippines.


128 Kidlat Logistics Services Co. was established in October 2020 it is the First ever delivery and services mobile app in Bulacan. Under its brand name are the follow services offered;
